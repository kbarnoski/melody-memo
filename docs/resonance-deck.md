## SLIDE 2: The Problem

**Musicians capture ideas. Then forget them.**

Hundreds of recordings pile up unnamed in phone folders. "What key was that in?" "What were those chords?" No way to search, compare, or develop ideas without sitting back down at the instrument.

Creative output goes unanalyzed, undeveloped, lost.

*The gap between capturing an idea and understanding it is where songs go to die.*

---

## SLIDE 3: The Solution

**Resonance makes every recording self-aware.**

Upload a musical recording. Resonance transcribes it, detects the key, chords, tempo, and progressions — then gives you an AI music theory coach to help develop it.

But understanding is only half of the experience. Resonance also transforms your music into something you can *inhabit* — immersive visual worlds that respond to the harmonic DNA of what you've played.

**Two connected experiences. One creative home.**

## SLIDE 6: The Room — What's Shipped

**200+ Shader Visualizations** — Six categories: Elemental, Visionary, Cosmic, Organic, Geometry, Dark — plus 3D worlds (aurora, galaxy, bonfire, crystal). Smooth crossfades between modes.

**Journey System** — 240+ built-in journeys across 16 realms (Heaven, Ocean, Winter, Temple, Cosmos...). Each journey unfolds in six phases — Threshold, Expansion, Transcendence, Illumination, Return, Integration — with multi-layer shader compositing, palette theming, and a curated emotional arc.

**AI Poetry & Voice** — Real-time poetic text overlays generated by AI, synced to the music's mood and phase. Whisper voice narration. 12 languages. 35 creative writing angles that rotate to stay fresh.

**Custom Journeys** — Create your own from a story prompt, or auto-generate from audio analysis. AI maps your music's mood to a realm, then composes the full visual arc.

**Shareable Rooms** — Public links to any journey. Anyone can experience your music without logging in.

## SLIDE 7: Installation & Performance Mode

**Resonance is not just software. It's a medium.**

The Room includes a dedicated installation mode designed for physical spaces — galleries, lobbies, performance venues, studios, and public environments.

**How it works:**
- Auto-plays a curated queue of recordings with synchronized visualizations
- Journeys unfold continuously — multi-layer shader compositing, poetry, narrative
- Cursor hides after 3 seconds, keyboard locked (kiosk mode)
- Full-screen, no UI chrome — just music and visuals
- Configure via URL: `/room/installation?journey=<id>`
- Loop indefinitely with queue wrapping

**Use cases:**

*Gallery Installation* — A pianist's recordings visualized as living art. Visitors walk into a room where music creates its own visual world. Each journey tells a story through light, color, and whispered language.

*Live Performance Backdrop* — A musician performs while The Room generates real-time visuals behind them. Shaders respond to the sonic environment, creating a unique visual experience for every performance.

*Meditation & Healing Spaces* — Spa lobbies, therapy offices, yoga studios. Ambient music with gently evolving visuals and optional whispered poetry. A calm, immersive background presence.

*Retail & Hospitality* — Hotels, restaurants, boutiques. Curated sonic-visual environments that set a mood without demanding attention.

*Museum & Cultural Exhibits* — Pair historical recordings with visual journeys. A Chopin nocturne rendered through the "Winter" realm. A jazz improvisation through "Cosmos."

*Studio Waiting Rooms* — Recording studios, music schools, creative agencies. Showcase original music as a visual experience while clients wait.

## SLIDE 9: Technology

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|-----------|
| Frontend | Next.js 15, React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind v4 |
| UI | shadcn/ui, Geist font family |
| AI | Claude (Anthropic) via Vercel AI SDK |
| Audio Intelligence | Spotify Basic Pitch (TensorFlow.js), tonal.js |
| Visualization | WebGL (200+ custom shaders), Three.js (3D worlds) |
| Audio Engine | Web Audio API, global singleton, WaveSurfer.js |
| Image Generation | FAL Flux (real-time AI imagery during journeys) |
| Backend | Supabase (Auth, PostgreSQL, Storage, RLS) |
| Audio Processing | FFmpeg (ALAC→AAC transcoding, range streaming) |
| Hosting | Vercel (serverless, edge) |

**Architecture highlights:**
- Global audio engine singleton survives route changes — seamless navigation between Studio and The Room
- Client-side analysis pipeline — no audio sent to servers
- 16 curated realms with visual vocabulary, palettes, and shader pools
- Journey engine manages phase transitions, shader rotation, and timing independently of React

## SLIDE 11: North Star Vision

**The musical brain you've always wanted.**

Imagine picking up your instrument. You play something — 30 seconds, two minutes, whatever comes out. Before you even set it down:

- The chords appear on screen in real time
- The app recognizes this is a variation of something you played three months ago
- It suggests: "This is in the same harmonic family as your piece 'Late November.' Try modulating to Eb at bar 8 — here's how it would sound."
- It generates a bass line, a string arrangement, and a drum pattern that fit your style
- Your teacher gets notified: "Karel explored tritone substitutions for the first time today"

And when you're done analyzing — you step into The Room. Your music becomes a world. Shaders paint the harmonic structure in light. Poetry whispers what the chords are saying. A journey unfolds — Threshold, Expansion, Transcendence — carrying you through the emotional arc of what you just played.

You share the link. A friend opens it on their phone. They're *inside* your music.

**Resonance is the AI co-composer that knows your entire musical history, understands your harmonic vocabulary, anticipates where you want to go, and helps you get there — then transforms the result into something you can see, feel, and share.**

Not a DAW. Not a notation app. Not a screensaver.

*A creative partner that turns every idea into its best possible version — and every performance into an experience.*
